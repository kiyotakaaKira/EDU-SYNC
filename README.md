## 📌 Overview

**EduSync** is an AI-powered employability and verified-talent ecosystem designed to bridge the gap between **students, alumni, and recruiters**.

Traditional career platforms treat resume analysis, skill verification, certifications, referrals, and recruitment as disconnected processes.

EduSync brings them together through a unified, evidence-backed **Career Intelligence Layer**.

Students can understand how well they fit a specific job, identify missing skills, analyze whether their credentials are actually relevant to that opportunity, build evidence-backed career profiles, generate job-specific Referral Passports, and connect with relevant alumni.

Recruiters can move beyond traditional resume filtering and discover candidates through **opportunity-specific, explainable talent matching**.

The complete journey:

```text
Resume Intelligence
        ↓
Skill Gap Analysis
        ↓
CredentialFit AI
        ↓
Evidence-Backed Career Profile
        ↓
Opportunity Intelligence
        ↓
Referral Passport
        ↓
Alumni Matching
        ↓
Trusted Referral
        ↓
Reverse Verified Talent Discovery
        ↓
Hiring Pipeline
```

# 🎯 Problem Statement

The modern employability ecosystem faces three major challenges.

### For Students

Students often:

- Apply to jobs without understanding their actual readiness.
- Cannot clearly identify the skills missing from their profiles.
- Collect certificates without knowing whether they matter for their target careers.
- Claim skills without strong supporting evidence.
- Struggle to find relevant alumni for genuine referrals.
- Apply repeatedly without understanding why they are being rejected.

### For Alumni

Alumni frequently receive referral requests without enough information to determine:

- Whether the candidate is actually suitable for the role.
- Whether their claimed skills have supporting evidence.
- Whether their credentials are relevant.
- Whether the candidate is truly referral-ready.

### For Recruiters

Recruiters face:

- Large volumes of resumes.
- Keyword-dependent screening.
- Unverified skill claims.
- Credentials with unclear job relevance.
- Difficulty discovering strong candidates who have not directly applied.

EduSync addresses these challenges through one connected platform.

## 🏆 2. CredentialFit AI

**CredentialFit AI** is one of EduSync's primary innovations.

Traditional certificate systems ask:

> Is this certificate valid?

EduSync asks:

> Is this credential trustworthy?

and:

> Does this credential actually matter for the job you want?

CredentialFit separates two important concepts:

```text
CREDENTIAL TRUST
        +
JOB-SPECIFIC RELEVANCE
```

Example:

```text
AWS Cloud Credential

Verification
VERIFIED

Target Opportunity
AI Engineer

Job Relevance
92%

Impact
HIGH
```

The same credential could produce:

```text
Target Opportunity
UI/UX Designer

Job Relevance
21%

Impact
LOW
```

A credential can therefore be **authentic but irrelevant** to a particular opportunity.

### Verification States

- `VERIFIED`
- `PARTIALLY VERIFIED`
- `UNABLE TO VERIFY`

### Readiness Impact

- `HIGH`
- `MEDIUM`
- `LOW`

- Skills

- Experience
- Career Readiness

Skills are represented using an evidence model.

### Skill Evidence Levels

```text
CLAIMED
   ↓
CREDENTIALLED
   ↓
DEMONSTRATED
   ↓
VALIDATED
```

### Example

| Skill | Evidence |
|---|---|
| Python | VALIDATED |
| Machine Learning | VALIDATED |
| FastAPI | DEMONSTRATED |
| AWS | CREDENTIALLED |
| CI/CD Pipelines | CLAIMED |

This shifts candidate profiles from **skill claims** toward **evidence-backed capability**.

## 🪪 6. EduSync Referral Passport

The Referral Passport transforms how students request referrals.

Instead of sending:

> "Hi, can you refer me?"

EduSync allows students to present an evidence-backed, job-specific profile.

The Referral Passport contains:

- Candidate Identity
- Target Opportunity
- Opportunity Fit
- Verified Skill Fit
- Credential Relevance
- Project Relevance
- Skill Evidence

Candidates are classified as:

```text
REFERRAL READY
```

or:

```text
IMPROVEMENT RECOMMENDED
```

The Referral Passport explicitly represents readiness for **one specific opportunity**.

It is never presented as a universal candidate score.

## 📈 10. Explainable Candidate Ranking

EduSync avoids unexplained black-box candidate rankings.

```text

+
20% Verified Skill Evidence
+
15% Credential Relevance
+
15% Project Relevance
+

+
5% Profile Completeness
```

AI is used to understand unstructured information.


# 🔐 Security

EduSync follows several basic security principles:

- Environment variables are excluded from source control.
- API keys remain server-side.
- Authentication infrastructure uses Supabase.
- AI responses are validated and handled with fallbacks.
- Credential verification avoids unsupported authenticity claims.
- Candidate ranking is opportunity-specific rather than presented as universal candidate quality.
- Sensitive candidate information should be protected through appropriate authorization policies in production deployments.

---

# 📊 Opportunity Fit Model

The MVP scoring model uses multiple career signals.

| Factor | Weight |
|---|---:|
| Skill Match | 35% |
| Verified Skill Evidence | 20% |
| Credential Relevance | 15% |
| Project Relevance | 15% |
| Experience Match | 10% |
| Profile Completeness | 5% |

The model is designed to provide transparent and explainable opportunity matching.

> Production deployments should continuously evaluate ranking quality, fairness, bias, accessibility, and job relevance. Protected personal characteristics should never be used as ranking signals.
